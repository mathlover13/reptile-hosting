Who We Are

What We Do

Spizzirri's Reptile Rescue & Rehabilitation is a non-for profit organization that is based in Kankakee county, Illinois. We rescue reptiles that can no longer be cared for due to unfortunate circumstances. The exotic animals in our care are placed in eviornments that promote the most stress free rehabilitation. Incoming animals are evaluated on a case by case basis to accomidate exotics with pre-exsisting health conditions. With our rescues, we educate the public through events and shows.
