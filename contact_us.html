How to Contact Us

The best was to contact us is via phone. You can call us at 708-704-9911. You can also reach out to us over email at Spizzirrireptilerescue@gmail.com We look forward to speaking with you.

Who You Are Contacting

Niko Spizzirri is our Lead Ambassador that started this organization to save exotic animals and educate the public about them. He devotes his freetime and works around the clock to care for the exotics at the facility. Niko has years of experience working with and taking care of animals ranging from guinea pigs to crocodiles. The educational presentations given at events will be carried out by Niko and the exotics from our rescue.

Exotic Rescue

Do you need to surrender your exotic(s) due to unfortunate circumstances? Do you know of exotics in need of rescue and rehabilitation? Please call 708-704-9911 to get the rescue process started. You will need to fill out procedural forms before we can accept any animal(s) into the facilty. Please transport your animal in their current cage. Additionaly, bring all of the items used to care for and house the animal(s). We will need to use the current enclosure(s) as temporary housing upon arrival.
